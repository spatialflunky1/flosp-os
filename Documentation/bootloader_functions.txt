List of 16 bit functions available to the bootloader (func16.inc):

Real mode functions:
---------------------
1: print_16(bx:message(addr))
2: disk_load(bx:memory_offset(addr), al:read_count, dl:drive_num, es:memory_segment(addr))
- loads the first `al` sectors of the booted drive

Protected mode functions:
--------------------------
1: print_32(ebx:message(addr))
2: detect_64()
3: enable_a20()

Long mode functions:
---------------------
1: print_64(rbx:message(addr))
